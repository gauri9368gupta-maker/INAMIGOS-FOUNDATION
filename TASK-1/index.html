WHO WE ARE

About InAmigos Foundation

Empowering lives through compassion, education, sustainability, and community service.

Together We Can Make Every Life Better.

InAmigos Foundation is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization established in 2020 with a vision of creating a compassionate and empowered society. The foundation works across India through impactful initiatives focused on education, women empowerment, environmental conservation, food distribution, animal welfare, and skill development.

We believe that sustainable development begins with community participation. Every volunteer, donor, and supporter plays a crucial role in transforming lives and creating opportunities for those who need them the most.

Our Mission

To uplift communities by providing education, resources, and opportunities that create long-term social impact.

Our Vision

Building an inclusive society where every individual has equal access to education, dignity, and growth.
